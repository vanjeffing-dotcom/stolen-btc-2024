## Statement of facts (official wording, translated)

> "On or around 5 November 2024, the suspect, without authorization, entered commands into the victim's Bitcoin electronic wallet, **withdrew 6.318566 BTC**, and thereby obtained pecuniary gain."

## Decision type

**Investigation suspended (Suspect-suspension — suspect not entered into system)**
— i.e. the case is not dismissed; it is held pending suspect identification.

## Reasons for suspension (verbatim, translated)

> 1. It is **established as fact** that the suspect stole the victim's mnemonic information and extracted the Bitcoin virtual assets from the victim's electronic wallet, thereby obtaining pecuniary gain.
>
> 2. Despite investigative measures including **tracing of the electronic wallet** used in the commission of this crime, **analysis of the victim's e-mail access logs**, and **international cooperative investigation**, the suspect has not been identified.
>
> 3. We have decided to suspend the investigation (suspect-suspension — suspect not in system) **until the suspect can be identified**.
